Farmer's Market

Mixed-Use · 2nd Year Studio

Project Overview

A proposal for a hybrid farmer's market and commercial center in Florida City that serves as an expression of civic pride. The design provides local growers from the Redlands with opportunities for value-added sales while giving the community affordable access to healthy food. In addition to the market, the program includes complementary commercial spaces that strengthen the site as a cultural hub.

Location Florida City, FL
Program Farmer's Market, Supermarket, Cafe
Gym, Offices, Youth Center
Tools AutoCAD, Rhino, Adobe Suite
