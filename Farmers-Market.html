Farmer's Market

Mixed-Use · 2nd Year Studio

Project Overview

A mixed-use development featuring a farmer's market with residential and commercial spaces. The design creates a vibrant community hub that supports local agriculture while providing housing and retail opportunities in an urban context.

Layered podium and tower volumes with active edges and mid-block public space create a dynamic urban environment that connects residents with local food systems.

  • Location Miami, FL
  • Program Mixed-Use
  • Tools AutoCAD, Rhino, Adobe Suite<
