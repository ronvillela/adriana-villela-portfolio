Interpretive Center

Cultural · 3rd Year Studio

Project Overview

A community-focused skate park integrating landscape, street plazas, and bowl features. The proposal balances spectator terraces with safe circulation, emphasizing porosity, shade, and night use through a minimal lighting strategy.

The section-driven approach sculpts the terrain to separate fast lines from gathering pockets, while maintaining clear sightlines across the park.

  • Location Miami, FL
  • Year 2024
  • Program Public Recreation
  • Tools Rhino, SketchUp, Enscape, Adobe Suite
